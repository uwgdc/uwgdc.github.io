# Welcome to UW Gamedev!

In case you're new here, we're a student-led game development group at the University of Waterloo. We don't gate membership, so you're already part of the club! Congrats! 🎉

On this site, we publish gamedev resources, guides, and [presentations](/blog) from members and guest speakers alike. Elsewhere, we provide communal spaces for our members.

## Goals and Values

- Guiding game developers at every stage
- Remaining engine-agnostic _(despite our Godot workshops)_
- Welcoming everyone capable of politeness

## Events

Events are hosted both in-person at the University of Waterloo, and through our [Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/tHEXh9d). Join it to stay up-to-date!

### General Meetings _— Thursdays @ 5-7 PM_

Home to most of our presentations and attendees, our Thursday meetings are our most popular offering.

### Game Potlucks — _Saturdays @ 12-2 PM_

Our laid-back potlucks are not for eating food, but for playing games! _Though bringing food is much appreciated._

### Game Jams — _One Per Term_

Every term, we host a game jam for our members and guests alike. Fun fact: we host the largest student-led jams in Canada.
